Did you know?

In Norse mythology a squirrel named "Ratatoskr" Was the carrier of information and would climb both up and down the tree of life, to deliver news from the eagle up top and the serpant who would dwell below near the roots of the tree of life.

Today they are known alittle different

Today most know squirrels as "bird feeder raiders", besides them being those critters you see at the parks running up and down the tree's, most people are more familiar with them being a big culprit as to why their newly hung bird feeders are either empty or even knocked down all together. Their antics and energy are undeniable, however did you know they are also natures "arborists"? at one point or another we have all heard the term "squirrel away" when refering to tucking something away perhaps for a rainy day. Squirrels do this all day (after they get their fill) they take nuts and cache them, somewhere safe for whenever they may need them. Well Did you know? that squirrels plant millions of trees every year simply because they cache a nut and just forget where they put it. Infact! chestnut tree's heavily rely on squrriels alone for this very reason (you can read more on that here). Well with deforestation and the constant expanding of the suburbs are losing their homes due to trees being cut down, some even become orphaned due to cars hitting many squirrels, which leaves up to 2-4 kits per litter helpless... This is where we come in.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple, we are here to offer a platform where we are able to rehabilitat direct people to licensed rehabilitaters in their area to help these littl guys and girls. We also offer an avenue for people to "adopt a squirrel" where people are able to donate money and recieve a picture (email or physical) on which little buddy they are aiding. We also have a small merch section where all the funds are directed to the aid of our squirrels which keeps then fed and housed.

Meet The Squirrels

Name: Crazy Girl
Age: 2
About: Crazy girl is a very lively, dominant female who first got her name because as a kit she was the most playful of the bunch, although she does still play she is known as an "alpha" squirrel.

Name: Momma
Age: 4
About: Momma squirrel is one of our oldest squirrels, yes her name came about because she has had several litters and was known to bring how babies to the house of the fine folks who first learned about her. She is a very sweet docile squirrel who knows how to charm people out of every walnut they have.

Name: Dougie
Age: 2
About: Dougie is actually the native squirrel in Washington state, he is a douglas squirrel which is lumped under the "red squirrel" name. Although he is about 2/3s the size of the average grey squirrel. Dougie is very lively and can be territorial, he is not afraid to throw his little weight around to defend his pile of walnuts. When he is unhappy you will hear about it by way of his squeeks
